3D Modeling and Animation

This 3D modeling and animation project was done using OpenGL. Images of a bunny, dragon and bear were constructed and rendered. The 3D objects were then colored using vector normals and matrix algebra.

The result is a stunning display of pixels and points that mesh together to form 3D objects. Animation is also present for each image, allowing the user to control its orientation, as well as its position in the portal. Each image rotates through time.

Music App - Refactoring

This project was based around the remodeling and refactoring of a music playlist app. Over the course of 4 weeks, my team of 4 conducted interviews with 9 students to determine the elements of major music apps (such as Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud) which were both favorable and unfavorable. We collected data on the types of Design that these interviewees liked, to use as the basis for our redesign.

We then conducted interviews in which partipants were asked to run through a particular scenario associated with popular music playlist apps. From this, we garnered which elements of these apps were intuitive, and which elements could be redesigned and refactored.

Once our scenarios were complete, we performed competitive analysis of information structures, before leaping into our design process. In the end, we created an app which dynamically combines personalized and exploratory features to aid users in an experience that is not only unique to them, but feels natural and intuitive as well. back.r

In our modern society, where people are more connected than ever through technology and social media, back.r is a tool that utilizes the power of connection in order to fuel people’s passion projects into tangible ideas. With multi-purpose, smart-matching, and social features, back.r focuses on creating a network of people, unrestrained by distance, to work together in a mutually beneficial manner. Users of our app will be able to crowdsource to a whole new world and demographic of people, unhampered by the needs that couldn’t be fulfilled by their location. back.r spurs a whole new generation of creativity and innovation without any restraint, igniting ideas.
